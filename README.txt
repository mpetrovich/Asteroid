ASTEROID

A 3D particle simulator I wrote back in college. It uses my "CUB3D" 3D rendering engine and true gravitational physics.

CONTROLS

Rotate camera = Click + Drag
Zoom in/out = Scroll up/down
Move camera = Shift Click + Drag

BUILDING

To compile:
javac *.java */*.java

To bundle into an executable JAR file:
jar cvfm Asteroid.jar Manifest.txt *.class */*.class
